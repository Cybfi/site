Cybfi

Cybfi is dedicated to protecting children.

I, founder of Cybfi - William Kenzie, am dedicated to putting protection before profit. My goal is to make parental control easier to use, cheaper, but most importantly - more sensible. Conventional parental control uses very little AI and worsens the relationship between a parent and their child. Our methods increases communication and mutual understanding, preventing the child from attempting to circumvent our software and enforces rules even when they do.

Current Projects

Cyber

Cyber is a parental control application designed to intelligently monitor child behavior and prevent misbehavior with solid communication, building respect and trust at a root level.

Asher

Asher is an anti-suicide chatbot designed to calm a person-in-crisis down using proven methods. Asher can also be embedded in any project, potentially expending it's reach.

SmallTalk

SmallTalk is a Cybfi-sponsored chatroom with the intent to provide Cyber's AI with a good data sample to learn from, while providing strict moderation. SmallTalk is aimed at all ages, so long as all ages are willing to behave as though their grandmother was logged on.
